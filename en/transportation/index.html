Transportation in San Carlos

Complete guide to getting around - car rentals, taxis, buses, and what you need to know

San Carlos is a destination where having your own transportation makes all the difference, as distances between beaches, viewpoints, restaurants, and activities can be long. Here's a clear and practical guide to all your options.

üöô 1. Rent a Car in San Carlos or Guaymas (Best Option)

The most comfortable way to get around San Carlos is to rent a car, especially if you plan to visit multiple beaches, viewpoints, or activities on a flexible schedule.

Where to Rent a Car

Major Agencies in Guaymas

Large rental companies (Hertz, Budget, Avis) are located in Guaymas, about 10-15 minutes from San Carlos.

  • Well-established brands
  • Wide selection of vehicles
  • Online reservations available
  • Short drive from San Carlos

Local Rentals in San Carlos

Small local businesses offer cars, vans, or side-by-sides (UTVs).

  • Convenient location in San Carlos
  • Personalized service
  • Book in advance during high season
Why Rent a Car?
  • Complete freedom to explore beaches and viewpoints
  • More economical than relying on taxis for the entire trip
  • Best option for families or groups
  • Ideal for visiting Scenic Viewpoint, Playa Los Algodones, Nacapule, Tecalai, etc.
