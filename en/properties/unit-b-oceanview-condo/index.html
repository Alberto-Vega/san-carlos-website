About this place

Welcome to Unit B, a centrally-located oceanview apartment in the heart of San Carlos! This charming vacation rental offers the perfect combination of stunning ocean vistas and resort-style amenities.


Enjoy breathtaking ocean views from your private patio, take a refreshing dip in the swimming pool, or relax at the palapa bar. For the active guests, the property features a pickleball court for friendly competitions and fun exercise.


This apartment sleeps up to 4 guests comfortably, making it ideal for couples, small families, or friends traveling together. The centrally-located position means you're close to San Carlos' best beaches, restaurants, and attractions while enjoying a peaceful retreat.


Unit B is part of a two-unit property, offering privacy while sharing access to exceptional amenities including the pool, palapa bar, and pickleball court. Experience the beauty of the Sea of Cortez from your own private oceanview paradise.
