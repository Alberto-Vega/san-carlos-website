San Carlos

A paradise to discover in the Sea of Cortez

Journey to San Carlos, where crystal-clear waters meet golden desert landscapes. Nestled on Sonora's stunning coastline, it offers an unmatched blend of natural beauty, world-class sport fishing, and authentic Mexican charm.

Pristine Beaches

Where desert meets the sea

Discover miles of unspoiled coastline, hidden coves, and turquoise waters perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and relaxation under the Mexican sun.

Marine Adventures

The aquarium of the world

Experience incredible diving and snorkeling in Jacques Cousteau's "Aquarium of the World," home to dolphins, sea lions, manta rays, and vibrant coral reefs.

San Carlos

Discover Desert & Coastal Trails

Explore the hiking trails of San Carlos to uncover breathtaking views of the Sea of Cortez and surrounding Sonoran Desert. Trek through dramatic rock formations, discover hidden beaches, and witness stunning sunrises over Tetakawi Peak.

Authentic Flavors

Taste the soul of Sonora

Indulge in fresh seafood caught daily from the Sea of Cortez, traditional Sonoran cuisine, and local specialties. From beachfront ceviche to authentic carne asada, experience the rich culinary heritage that makes San Carlos a food lover's paradise.
